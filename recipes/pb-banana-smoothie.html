Peanut Butter and Banana Smoothie

Description

This beverage is a simple and healthy smoothie made with peanut butter and banana.

Ingredients

  • 1 Frozen banana
  • 1 Scoop peanut butter
  • 8 oz unflavoured Kefir or similar yogurt
  • 8 oz milk

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to blender.
  2. Blend. Use frozen drink mode is available.
  3. Serve immediately in a glass.
