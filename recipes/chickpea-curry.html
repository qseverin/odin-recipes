Chickpea Curry

Description

This dish is a simple vegan curry made with chickpeas and spinach. It is seasoned with fresh onion, garlic, ginger, and curry powder.

Ingredients

  • Olive Olive
  • Curry powder
  • 1 Yellow onion
  • 2 Cloves garlic
  • 1 Inch ginger root
  • 2 Cans chickpeas
  • 1 Can tomato sauce
  • 8 oz spinach

Steps

  1. Peel ginger. Mince ginger and garlic. Dice onion.
  2. Saute ginger, garlic, and onion in a skillet until onion is transluscent.
  3. Add spinach with 1/4 cup water. Add curry powder. Cook until spinach is steamed.
  4. Drain and rinse chickpeas. Add chickpeas and tomato sauce. Stir well. Cook until chickpeas begin to soften.
